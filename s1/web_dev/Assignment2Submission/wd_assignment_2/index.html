Our Ice-Cream

Our ice-creams are made with the finest fresh ingredients found locally and do not include any preservatives, artificial food colourings or flavours. Our flavours include popular favorites as well as some seasonal specialties. No doubt we have a flavour for everyone!

Our History

Sweet Life ice-cream store began in 2012. Since then this family owned and operated business has been working hard to provide the community with the best ice-cream delights we possibly can. We put our heart and soul into each ice-cream we create to provide sweet joy and sweet happiness in every mouthful. We like to give back to the community that supports us so well, by sponsoring local junior football, soccer and hockey teams in the area.
